Labarin sarki da waziri: A wani gari an taba yin wani sarki mai tarin dukiya
sai dai allah ya jarrabeshi da rashin da, yana da waziri shedanin waziri.
wata rana ya shawarci wazirin nashi da ya tara mishi kaf malaman garin don
wannan abu da ya dameshi, aka yi taro iya taro ana jiran fitowar sarki, can bada
jimawa ba sai ga sarki tare da waziri yana take mishi baya, duk inda ka duba dakaru kake gani

Sarki ya hau karagar mulki ya zauna jama'a suka fadi suka yi gaisuwa, sarki ya dubi
taron jama'ar nan babu masakar tsinke, sai yace a ransa yanzu wannan dandazon al'ummar
kaf a karkashin mulkina suke amma baza su iya samamun da ko guda ba, bai san sanda
maganar zuci ta fito fili ba, yace kai allah gwani! waziri ya daga kai ya dubi sarki
yace: ranka dade Allah ja zamani jamaa sun taru anji kayi shiru, harda subutar baki
ko lafiya?

Sarki yace: ba komai waziri, sai ya fara bayani: yaku al'ummar wannan gari mai albarka
na Gandumu, ko kun san dalilin wannan taro? jamaa suka ce: Allah ja zamanin ka sai ka fada
sarki yace, kun san wannan matsala data addabe ni na rashin da! ba irin tambaya da maganin
da ba a gwada ba, shine na yanke shawara na tara ku dan inyi gamammiyar shela, na cewa da
cikin wannan birni nawan da wajen shi duk wanda yamin dalilin samun da nayi alkawari zan
bashi rabin mulkina! ai waziri sai yaji kamar an lafta mishi mari
